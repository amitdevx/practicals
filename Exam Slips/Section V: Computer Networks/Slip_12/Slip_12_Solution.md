# Slip 12 — Computer Networks Solution Guide

## Q1: Short Answer Questions

### a) What are modem types?

**Modem Types:**
- **Dial-up**: Telephone lines, 56 Kbps
- **DSL**: Over phone lines, up to 100 Mbps
- **Cable**: Over cable TV, up to 1 Gbps
- **Fiber**: Fiber optic, up to 10 Gbps

---

### b) What is packet structure?

**Packet Components:**
- Header: Source/destination, protocol
- Payload: Actual data
- Trailer: Error checking (CRC)

---

### c) What are ICMP messages?

**ICMP Message Types:**
- Echo Request/Reply (Ping)
- Destination Unreachable
- Time Exceeded
- Redirect
- Parameter Problem

---

### d) Network simulation tools.

**Tools:**
- Cisco Packet Tracer
- GNS3
- Mininet
- EVE-NG

---

### f) What is port forwarding?

**Port Forwarding:** Redirects external port to internal device.

```
External: 192.168.1.1:8080
↓
Internal: 192.168.100.10:80
```

---

### g) What is bandwidth allocation?

**Bandwidth Management:**
- QoS (Quality of Service)
- Traffic shaping
- Rate limiting
- Priority queuing
